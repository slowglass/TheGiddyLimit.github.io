Initiative Tracker Player View

Press F to toggle fullscreen. Good luck.

    The Player View is part of a peer-to-peer (i.e., serverless) system to allow players to connect to a DM's DM Screen initiative tracker. As a player, the usage is as follows:

    1. Paste a "server token," provided by a DM, into the "Server Token" field, and click "Generate Client Token."
    2. Wait for a token to be appear in the "Client Token" field, copy it, and send it to the DM.

    Once the DM accepts your token, this page will change to display the encounter in the DM's tracker. Please note that this system is highly experimental. Your experience may vary.
